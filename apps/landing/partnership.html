Partnership Levels

Support That Matches Your Intent

Schools differ in size, structure, and readiness. Our partnership levels reflect how much ongoing alignment and guidance a school wants during the year — not just access to tools.

Two Components, One Partnership

Every partnership includes two parts: an annual school partnership covering culture design, training, and advisory support — and per-student platform access for daily use of the apps.

The annual partnership reflects how intentionally a school wants to steward culture. The per-student cost simply scales with enrollment.

"What level is right for us?" usually comes down to one question: Do you want to carry alignment forward independently, or do you want structured guidance throughout the year?

What You're Getting

Understanding the Components

Staff Orientation & Training

Beginning-of-year sessions that establish shared vocabulary, explain the framework philosophy, and align staff around consistent expectations. Not a one-time presentation — a foundation for how adults talk about growth.

Silver: BOY only · Gold: BOY + semester follow-ups · Platinum: + targeted team sessions

Leadership Check-in Calls

Scheduled conversations with school leadership to reflect on implementation, surface challenges, and adjust direction. These calls ensure the framework adapts to your school's reality rather than becoming a static system.

Silver: Not included · Gold: 1 per semester · Platinum: Up to 3 per semester

Student Insight Cards (SICs)

Structured reflection tools that help staff have meaningful conversations with students about growth, behaviour, and character. Not performance reviews — prompts for genuine formation dialogue.

Silver: Not included · Gold & Platinum: Included

3Rs Curriculum

A structured curriculum for deeper character formation work. Implementation guidance included to ensure it integrates with your existing schedule and approach rather than competing with it.

Silver & Gold: Not included · Platinum: Available for $2,500/year

Which Partnership Fits Your School?

"We're piloting this for the first time and want to start simple."

Silver gives you the foundation without overwhelming your staff. You can always deepen the partnership in year two.

"We've tried culture initiatives before that faded by November."

Gold exists precisely for this. The semester follow-ups and check-in calls keep alignment alive when the initial energy naturally fades.

"We're growing fast and need help maintaining consistency across campuses or divisions."

Platinum provides the advisory bandwidth to handle complexity — targeted training for different teams, more frequent touchpoints, and curriculum support.

"We're not sure yet."

That's expected. Most schools start with a conversation about where they are and where they want to go. We'll help you think through what makes sense.
