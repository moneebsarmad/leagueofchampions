Partnership Levels

Support That Matches Your Intent

Schools differ in size, structure, and readiness. Our partnership levels reflect how much ongoing alignment and guidance a school wants during the year — not just access to tools.

Three Components, One Partnership

League of Champions is not just software access. It’s a culture partnership with three parts:

• Onboarding (setup + staff alignment)
• Ongoing stewardship (touchpoints that keep consistency alive)
• LOC App suite (daily visibility for students, staff, and families)

Most schools don’t need more initiatives. They need alignment that survives the semester.

Two Levels

Choose Based on Stewardship

Every level includes the full platform. What differs is the rhythm of alignment.

Tier 1

Onboarding + Light Stewardship

Launch the system. Keep it steady.

For schools that want a clear launch, a shared staff language, and a small number of touchpoints to prevent drift.

What’s Included

  • Onboarding package (setup + staff training)
  • 2 leadership check-ins (year)
  • 1 staff calibration session (mid-year)
  • 2 implementation reviews (brief + action notes)

Best when you have an internal culture lead who can carry the weekly rhythm.

Most Common
Tier 2

Onboarding + Active Stewardship

More touchpoints. More consistency.

For schools that want tighter alignment across teachers, stronger follow-through, and more structured reinforcement as the year unfolds.

What’s Included

  • Everything in Tier 1
  • 4 leadership check-ins (year)
  • 2 staff follow-up sessions
  • 2 data reviews + action plan
  • 1 targeted team session (Deans / Advisors / Grade team)

Best when consistency is a priority across many classrooms or rapid growth.

Understanding the Components

Onboarding (included in both tiers)

Discovery + Culture Map (90 min)

Clarify the 3Rs language, staff habits to reinforce, and what consistency should look like across classrooms.

Platform Setup + Roster Import (3–5 hours)

Accounts, roles, house assignment, categories, and launch configuration.

Staff Orientation (3 hours)

Practice awarding, calibrate scenarios, and align on how praise/correction will sound.

Launch Support (2 hours)

Week 1 check-in, adjustments, and staff follow-through plan.

Tier 1 stewardship (annual)

Leadership check-ins (2 × 60 min)

Surface drift early, remove obstacles, and keep expectations stable.

Staff calibration (1 × 120 min)

Re-align language, address inconsistencies, and reset norms mid-year.

Implementation reviews (2 × 60 min)

Short written notes: what’s working, what’s drifting, what to reinforce next.

Tier 2 stewardship (annual)

Leadership check-ins (4 × 60 min)

Staff follow-ups (2 × 120 min)

Data review + action plan (2 × 90 min)

Patterns by grade/house/categories + next-step plan for staff consistency.

Targeted team session (1 × 120 min)

Deans, Advisors, or a grade team — focus on one pressure-point.
