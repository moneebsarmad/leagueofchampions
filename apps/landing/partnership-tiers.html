Partnership Levels

Support That Matches Your Intent

Schools differ in size, structure, and readiness. Our partnership levels reflect how much ongoing alignment and guidance a school wants during the year — not just access to tools. Stewardship means we don’t just hand over software — we guide, measure, correct, and sustain implementation until it becomes self-running.

Three Components, One Partnership

League of Champions is not just software access. It’s a culture partnership with three parts:

• Onboarding (setup + staff alignment)
• Ongoing stewardship (touchpoints that keep consistency alive)
• LOC App suite (daily visibility for students, staff, and families)

The shared language is the 3Rs — Respect, Responsibility, and Righteousness — used to name behavior, reinforce growth, and keep expectations consistent across classrooms.

Most schools don’t need more initiatives. They need alignment that survives the semester.
