The House System

Belonging That Builds Character

Every student belongs to a house. Every house embodies a virtue rooted in our tradition.

Loyalty

House of Abu Bakr

Unwavering in commitment. First to believe, first to support. Loyalty to Allah, community, and truth.

Wisdom

House of Khadijah

Discerning and decisive. Leading with insight, supporting with strength. Wisdom that uplifts others.

Moral Courage

House of Umar

Bold in justice. Speaking truth when it's hard, standing for what's right even when standing alone.

Creativity

House of Aishah

Curious and expressive. Using knowledge and creativity in service of the ummah. Innovation rooted in purpose.

House names can be adapted to fit your school's tradition. The structure remains the same.
