The House System

One System. Four Houses.

Houses create belonging. The 3Rs create clarity. Every student belongs to a house — and every adult reinforces the same language.

Champions of Ihsan

Character that holds under pressure — and shows up in service.

Core Virtues

Truthfulness Reliability Courage Service

Guiding Question

Who are our students becoming — when nobody is watching?

How it forms students

It gives staff a consistent way to notice growth, name it, and coach it — so expectations don’t reset every period.

The four houses

House of Abu Bakr

Anchors: Truthfulness

  • Tells the truth even when it’s uncomfortable
  • Protects trust and honesty in friendships

House of Umar

Anchors: Justice

  • Owns mistakes and makes things right
  • Uses strength to protect, not to intimidate

House of Khadijah

Anchors: Service

  • Helps without needing attention
  • Strengthens the community quietly and consistently

House of Aishah

Anchors: Knowledge

  • Asks better questions and seeks clarity
  • Uses learning to improve character — not ego

House names can be adapted to fit your school’s tradition. The purpose stays the same: belonging + formation.
